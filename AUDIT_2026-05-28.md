## Executive Summary

- **Bugs / smells found:** 22 in code, 2 in figure-generation pipeline.
- **Figures needing fix:** 4 broken (1 unregeneratable HotpotQA, 1 stale theta_density, 1 annotation-overflow predicted_vs_empirical, 1 mislabelled privacy_quality), 11 silently reliant on `scripts/regen_figures.py` (the `_find()` long-term fix was never applied).
- **Rerun-required:** 2 critical (HotpotQA sweep — §40 has no data on disk; DeepSeek + GPT-oss frontier validations — §38 numbers unsupported). 1 advisory (theorem validation with `full_validation_per_family`).
- **New experiments required (NeurIPS P0/P1):** Theorem 1 written proof, CAAC theta-sensitivity, CAAC N-sensitivity, GPT-4o spot check, Selective Context as 4th compressor.
- **Thesis claims currently at risk:** 4 — (a) §40 HotpotQA + Corollary 2 second external benchmark, (b) §38 multi-model frontier (only Qwen 72B is real), (c) CAAC "100% dominance" rests on a tied-coord criterion that counts compression-axis losses as wins, (d) "theta-aware" CAAC narrative is false in the deployed code path — `derive_theta()` is never called and theta=0.5 globally.
